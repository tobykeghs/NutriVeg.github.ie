milk allergy

Milk allergy is an abnormal response by the body's immune system to milk and products containing milk.

garlic allergy

Some people are allergic to raw garlic, when touched by the skin can cause dermatitis, but can tolerate eating cooked garlic.

soy allergy

Allergy to soy, a product of soybeans, is a common food allergy.

nut allergy

The most common symptom of a nut allergy is raised red bumps of skin and other allergic symptoms such as runny nose, cramps, nausea or vomiting.
