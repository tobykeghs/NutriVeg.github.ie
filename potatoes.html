INGREDIENTS

  • 2 TbpsOil ( vegitable or olive oil which ever on you prefer)
  • 1/2cTbps Salt

Steps

  • 1 .

    Pour water in the bowl depending on the amount of potatoes you want to wash.

  • 2 .

    Wash the potatoes.

  • 3 .

    pour the water out of the bowl, but leave the potatoe in the bowl.

  • 4 .

    Peel the potatoe using a Peeler.

  • 5 .

    Cut the potatoe to your prefered taste, but better to cut them into strips.

  • 6 .

    Season the potatoe with salt.

  • 7 .

    Place the frying pan on fire and pour oil in it, leaving it for 6 min until the oil get hot.

  • 8 .

    Pour the potatoe into the hot oil and leave it to fry for 20mins.

  • 9 .

    once the potatoe turns golden and crunchy it is ready.
