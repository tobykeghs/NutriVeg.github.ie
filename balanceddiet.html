A balanced diet is one that fulfills all of a person's general nutritional needs. Human beings need a certain amount of calories and nutrients a day to stay healthy. A balanced diet helps in balancing all the nutrients a person requires, without going over the recommended daily calorie intake. By eating a balanced diet, one can get the nutrients and calories they need and avoid eating junk food, or food that lack nutritional value. A poor diet is a major reason why people struggle with weight loss. that is why a balanced diet can help a person reduce their risk factors for obesity or gaining weight.

The 4 food groups

Vegetables

People may enjoy vegetables either raw or cooked. However, it is important to note that cooking vegetables removes some of their nutritional value. Also, some methods, such as frying, will add unhealthful fats to a dish.

Fruits

A balanced diet also involes plenty of fruit. Instead of getting fruit from juice, it is recommend that eating whole fruits is the best way to go.

Grains

Whole grains include all parts of the grain which three, they bran, germ, and endosperm. The body breaks down whole grains slowly, so they have little effect on a person's blood sugar.

Protein

Protein is so important relating ot every cell in the body. Hair and nails are made of protein. Your body needs protein to build and repair tissues.
