deep fried crispy chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 Tbsp.oli
  • Half tbsp Salt
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • chicken (drumstick or breast)

steps

  • 1 .

    rub the seasoning cubes and salt on the chicken breast for taste

  • 2 .

    pour oil in the pan, depening on the size of the breast. but the oil shouldnt cover the chicken because you are not deep frying.

  • 3 .

    place the pan on the burner for 3mins so the oil can heat up.

  • 4 .

    place the chicken in the oil and fry each size for 7min.

  • 5 .

    Once the chicken turns golden brown the chiken is ready.
